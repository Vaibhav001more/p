📖 Investment Glossary

SIP
Systematic Investment Plan — invest a fixed amount monthly automatically.
NAV
Net Asset Value — price of one unit of a mutual fund on any given day.
Compound Interest
Earning interest on your interest — the magic that makes wealth grow exponentially.
Diversification
Spreading money across different assets to reduce risk. Don't put all eggs in one basket.
P/E Ratio
Price-to-Earnings ratio — how much you're paying for ₹1 of a company's earnings.
Demat Account
Electronic account to hold shares. Required to invest in Indian stock market.
ELSS
Equity Linked Saving Scheme — tax-saving mutual fund with 3-year lock-in.
Bull & Bear Market
Bull = rising prices. Bear = falling prices. Normal cycles every investor must understand.
